ABOUT US

Counselity is a distinguished organisation, home to a team of expert psychologists specialising in various fields. Our roster includes clinical psychologists, counselling psychologists, hypnotherapists, child therapists, and trauma-informed therapists. With a collective commitment to mental well-being, our experts bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to address a wide spectrum of psychological needs.

Our services extend to encompass a diverse range of mental and emotional health concerns, such as depression, anxiety, relationship issues, work stress, trauma, and specific challenges related to children and adolescents. Whether you are navigating personal struggles, coping with work-related stressors, or seeking specialized support for your child, Counselity provides tailored interventions. Our holistic approach ensures that individuals receive comprehensive and effective assistance on their path to emotional well-being.

OUR STORY

In a world where stress and uncertainty often overshadow the pursuit of well-being, Counselity emerged from a shared vision and a deep-rooted desire for change. Founded by four individuals who witnessed firsthand the toll of unethical practices in their workplaces, Counselity is more than just a counseling center—it's a beacon of integrity, compassion, and empowerment.

Driven by a collective mission to create a safe haven for healing and growth, our founders—Aakriti, Madhavi, Mansi, and Sakshi—embarked on a journey fueled by passion and purpose. Having experienced the impact of unethical behavior on mental and emotional well-being, they recognized the urgent need for a holistic approach to healing—one that prioritizes empathy, authenticity, and ethical standards.

At Counselity, we believe that healing is not just about addressing symptoms; it's about addressing the root causes and creating sustainable pathways to growth. That's why our team of compassionate counselors and therapists is dedicated to providing personalized support tailored to each individual's unique needs and circumstances.
